🥗 Lunch — What's for lunch?

About

A lightweight progressive web app that suggests a random keto-friendly lunch idea based on where you are and what the weather is doing.

Features

📍

Detects your location and fetches live local weather

🌤️

Displays a weather emoji in the top-right corner — clear, cloudy, rain, snow, hot or freezing

🍗

Picks a random keto-friendly meal from a curated database (chicken, beef, seafood, lamb or pork)

📸

Shows a photo, the dish name, and a link to the full recipe

Gestures

👈

Swipe left on the card to dismiss it and load the next random suggestion

👉

Swipe right if it looks good — get a "Yummy!" reaction

How it works

Weather data comes from Open-Meteo (free, no account needed). Meal data and photos come from TheMealDB. No data is stored or sent anywhere.
